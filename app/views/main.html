Where: Portola Valley Town Center


When: Thursdays 3-7PM



Every Thursday afternoon, in the parking lot in front of the Historic Schoolhouse, surrounded by the ancient redwood trees, something magical happens. Quietly, one by one, the farmers begin to appear. Tents pop up, fruits and vegetables are unloaded onto tables. The baker arrives with his loaves warm from the oven. The coffee roaster unloads his freshly roasted beans. The sausage maker arrives, then the fishmonger, the farmstead meat vendor. The eggs appear on the table and trickling in, the customers arrive and get in line for the warm loaves. The laughter begins. Every Thursday, year-round. 3-7pm in summer, 2-5pm in winter.
